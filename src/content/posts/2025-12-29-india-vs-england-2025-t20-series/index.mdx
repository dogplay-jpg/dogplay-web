## Introduction to the Series
The India vs England 2025 T20 series will feature a total of 5 matches, with the first match scheduled to take place on February 10, 2025, at the Narendra Modi Stadium in Ahmedabad. The series will then move to other venues across India, including the M.Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru and the Eden Gardens in Kolkata.

### Schedule and Venues
The schedule for the India vs England 2025 T20 series is as follows:
- February 10, 2025: 1st T20I, Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad
- February 12, 2025: 2nd T20I, M.Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bengaluru
- February 15, 2025: 3rd T20I, Eden Gardens, Kolkata
- February 18, 2025: 4th T20I, Greenfield International Stadium, Thiruvananthapuram
- February 20, 2025: 5th T20I, Barsapara Cricket Stadium, Guwahati
